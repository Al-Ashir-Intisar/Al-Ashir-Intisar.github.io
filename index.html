Welcome to Our Website!

We have seen exponential growth in the use of AI in our everyday lives. But there is one aspect of life that has seen a delayed integration of AI tools due to ethical and privacy concerns which is Healthcare. The use of AI tools in health care such as Medical Imaging, Predictive Analytics, Personalized Medicine, Spatial Proteomics, etc. is still in its infancy. This is because healthcare data is very private and poses risks and threats if not protected well. The use of AI tools in healthcare also raises ethical considerations like how much decision-making can be allowed by AI and under which circumstances and if a wrong decision is made through the use of AI who is to be blamed. This is why this topic is relatively new and requires more research and exploration and simultaneous mass education. We are using our interactive website to showcase some of the privacy concerns of handling certain types of data, like the privacy levels that come in when dealing with certain types of information. Throughout this project, we will be able to educate people on having a better understanding when it comes to their privacy, and ethical issues when it comes to AI in health-related fields.
